Migrate ACES to GCP as an early move of a market that can be done without any multicast requirement.


Business Context

Objectives

  • Launch a matching market based on TCP connectivity without requiring customer colo.
  • Launch a matching market using pure cloud technologies that can start up in an alternate infrastructure in a weekend.
  • Support A / B deployment pattern for component groups that can migrate to alternate infrastructure independent of each other.

Background

ACES markets can be a leader amongst CME markets in moving to Google Cloud platform because it does not require external multicast, and because the low latency demands are relaxed compared to other CME markets. An early move of ACES assumes that the matching market can move without customers being co-located, implying that access latency can be potentially well above 10 milliseconds.
